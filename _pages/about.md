Hey there, welcome to my website!

I am a Research Consultant at the European Central Bank (ECB) and Research Assistant at the University of Copenhagen (UCPH). Previously, I have been a Research Assistant at the Center for Economic Behavior (CEBI).

My research focuses on the macro and micro effects of structural change, with a special focus on inequality and environmental implications. I have over two years of experience in empirical macro and microeconometrics. I am particularly interested in applying the theoretical and modelling skills I developed through coursework to concrete research questions.

Starting September 2026, I will join the Paris School of Economics as a master's student in the Analysis and Policy in Economics (APE) programme, jointly offered with [EHESS](https://www.parisschoolofeconomics.eu/glossaire/ehess/), [ENS–PSL](https://www.parisschoolofeconomics.eu/glossaire/ens-psl/), l'École nationale des ponts et chaussées, and l'université Paris 1 Panthéon-Sorbonne.

Here you can find my [CV](/files/Mathilde_Vallat_CV.pdf).

I am happy to share that my first paper "Estimating the Economic Costs of Extreme Weather in Real Time" received a Revise & Resubmit from Nature Communications Earth and Environment.
